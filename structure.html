The primary goal of databases

There are combinations of databases according to the type of mathematical relationship between the data, including:

Hierarchical structure
It is the adoption of a pyramid relationship between data elements, such that each element is responsible for only one element and not more.
Relational structure
It is the adoption of a specific relationship between data elements, such that the value of an element depends on the sum of two elements. This structure is the most successful structure applied in the world of information databases, due to its diversity in the type of relationship between data, because the probability of implementing relationships in it is greater than any other structure.
Structural structure
: It is the adoption of the relationship of the organizational structure between the data elements, such that two elements are classified under one component or subordinate to it.
